# Hexnity Platform

> **Bridge the gap between learning and professional experience.**

Hexnity is a non-profit platform built for developers who want to contribute to open-source projects and gain genuine, real-world experience. Built with **Laravel** and **Filament**, Hexnity serves as a bridge for developers to transition from academic learning to professional-grade software development.

---

## 🌟 The Vision

The biggest challenge for emerging developers is the "experience gap." Hexnity solves this by providing a structured environment where developers from beginner to senior levels can collaborate on meaningful projects.

### Why Hexnity?
* **Credit System:** Every commitment you make earns you credits. These aren't just points; they are a verifiable reflection of your technical growth and dedication.
* **Career Proof:** Showcase your Hexnity profile during interviews to prove your practical experience and remote collaboration skills.
* **Senior Feedback:** Gain invaluable insights through code reviews and feedback from senior developers.
* **Zero Cost:** Hexnity is a non-profit project. Anyone can contribute without fees. **Your contribution is the fee.**

---

## 🚀 Key Features

* **Curated Projects:** A wide range of open-source projects ranging from beginner-friendly tasks to complex senior-level architecture.
* **Filament-Powered Dashboard:** A sleek, intuitive management interface for tracking your progress and credits.
* **Remote Team Experience:** Learn the workflows of professional remote teams, including Git flow, pull requests, and peer reviews.
* **Professional Portfolio:** A dedicated profile page that serves as a living CV for your open-source journey.
